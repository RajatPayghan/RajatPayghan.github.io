About

My name is Rajat Payghan and I am a student at Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani. I am a newbie coder and programmer, and I enjoy learning new stuff and  improve my skills. I am currently learning the languages C++, C and JavaScript. I am passionate about technology and software and would love to contribute in the development of projects using my skillset. 

While in school, I enjoyed learning about and making music. I was an exemplary leader and good team worker in events organised by my school like the annual gatherings or sports meet. I also represented my school in various football tournaments organised across my city. I am enthusiastic and curious about science since my school days and I hope to learn more about it in my upcoming college years.
